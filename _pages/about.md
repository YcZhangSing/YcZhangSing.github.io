I am currently a research intern at the China Telecom Artificial Intelligence Research Institute (TeleAI), where I work on explainable forgery detection.

I received my bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Northwest A&F University. During this period, I worked with [Prof. Rui Mao](https://cie.nwsuaf.edu.cn/szdw/fjs/2008116098/index.htm) and [Prof. Meili Wang](https://cie.nwsuaf.edu.cn/szdw/js/2012110003/index.htm) on smart agriculture research.

I am currently pursuing my master's degree in Software Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University. I collaborate with [Prof. Li Zhu](https://gr.xjtu.edu.cn/web/zhuli), [Assoc. Prof. Yaxiong Wang](https://faculty.hfut.edu.cn/~bAb2ea/zh_CN/), and [Prof. Zhedong Zheng](https://www.zdzheng.xyz/) on multimodal content understanding, with a focus on misinformation detection and agent navigation.

My research interests include explainable forgery detection, multimodal misinformation understanding, and AI for intelligent agriculture.
